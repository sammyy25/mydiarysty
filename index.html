What are you writing for?

Writing a diary is a good way to make sure your memories and experiences stay alive.It lets you keep track

and reflect on your past and learn from your mistakes. It can also be tremendously therapeutic.Not only to

record fun and fun and adventurous moments, but also sad and scary times. It can be helpful

to be able to document changes in your life.

Privacy is our #1 Concern

Even when carefully kept, paper journals can be read by anyone who happens upon them.

Our focus is on security and privacy, and all diaries are private by default.

myDiary keeps your journals safe with double password protection and military

strength encryption so you can be rest assured knowing that your entries are

secure in myDiary Vault
